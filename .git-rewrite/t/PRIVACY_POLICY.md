# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

Welcome to AnexLK. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our boarding and rental listing platform. By using AnexLK, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address (used for authentication)
- Password (encrypted and stored securely)
- Name (if provided)
- Phone number (if provided by property owners)
- User role (student or property owner)

### 1.2 Listing Information
If you are a property owner, we collect:
- Property details (title, description, location)
- Property images
- Pricing information
- Contact details
- Property features and amenities

### 1.3 Reviews and Ratings
When you submit reviews, we collect:
- Rating scores (1-5 stars)
- Review text
- Your email address (masked for privacy in public display)

### 1.4 Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
- Location data (when you use map features)

### 1.5 Cookies and Sessions
We use cookies and session storage to:
- Keep you logged in
- Remember your preferences
- Track your browsing activity on our platform
- Improve site functionality

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Platform Functionality
- To create and manage your account
- To display your property listings
- To enable communication between students and owners
- To process and display reviews and ratings
- To provide map and location services

### 2.2 Authentication
- We use email and password authentication only
- Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text
- We do not offer third-party login options (no Google login, no OAuth)

### 2.3 Public Information
- Property listings are visible to all users
- Reviews and ratings are publicly displayed (with masked email addresses)
- Your name may be displayed on your listings or reviews

### 2.4 Service Improvement
- To analyze platform usage and improve user experience
- To fix technical issues
- To develop new features

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Supabase
We use Supabase for:
- Database storage
- User authentication
- File storage (property images)
- Supabase's privacy practices apply to data stored with them

### 3.2 Google Maps
We use Google Maps API for:
- Displaying property locations
- Map-based search and filtering
- Distance calculations
- Google's privacy policy applies to map-related data

### 3.3 Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. We only share data:
- With third-party services necessary for platform operation (Supabase, Google Maps)
- When required by Sri Lankan law
- To protect our rights or prevent fraud

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted password storage
- Secure data transmission (HTTPS)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

### 4.2 Security Disclaimer
While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You use our platform at your own risk.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Account Data
- We retain your account information while your account is active
- If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained longer if required by law

### 5.2 Listing Data
- Property listings remain visible until deleted by the owner
- Deleted listings are removed from public view immediately
- Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days

### 5.3 Review Data
- Reviews remain visible even if you delete your account
- Reviews may be retained for historical accuracy
